04Sharing & Disclosure

  • Legal: we may disclose data where required by law, court order, or governmental authority in India.
  • Business transfers: in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, data may be transferred subject to this Policy.

05Cross-Border Transfers

Some of our processors (e.g., AI model and cloud providers) may process data outside India. We transfer data only to jurisdictions permitted under the DPDP Act and applicable government notifications, with appropriate contractual safeguards.
